About Me

We can make it together

My name is Kyle Raymond and I run Raymond Plays Teaching. I have developed a passion for bass and guitar over the past 10 years and want to share it with others. I gained knowledge in many different genres of music, have experience in live performance and have accumulated a following on social media for my various covers.

I am also currently in Post-secondary, completing my Bachelor of Education at Queen's University and have been involved with different schools and companies over the past few years that assist in tutoring/teaching students. I have taught various subjects and can cater to the needs of the student to ensure a positive learning experience.

Learning guitar truly changed my life and gave me a creative platform to express myself in ways that other mediums couldn't. I strongly believe that when music is used as an outlet from the realities and complexities of everyday life, creativity is given the power to shine and make a positive impact. With the knowledge I have acquired in both guitar and teaching, I am well equipped to teach both guitar and bass guitar and give you the power to play.
